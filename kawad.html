FOOT MARCHING

Let's read about how my school's team made it to the state level !

Every year, our school send two representatives, the boys' and girls' teams to join foot marching competition. We took the bus. At the district level, the boys' and girls' teams surprisingly won first place. After that, we competed against other schools at the state level, where we encountered intense rivalry. The girls' squad ended up in fourth place because one of us unintentionally bumped into the cone that was there.
