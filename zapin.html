ZAPIN

Let's read up on how I can become a Zapin member !

At the age of 14, I joined zapin. One day, I heard that there will be a zapin training that evening in the hall. I didn't come out of interest, but rather because my friend asked me to try. We entered the hall without an interview after the on-duty teacher instructed us all to learn how to dance. Perhaps we were accepted because so few people attended the training. Ever since that day, we have joined in whenever the school has an event, such when the principal or a VIP visits. It seems that my sister joined the zapin as well, without recognizing it. Thus, at that time, we were siblings who joined Zapin. We train every day in the evening and night when we wish to compete in the district-level zapin competition. Without more delay, let's watch my state-level zapin performance at the Johor Heritage Centre in Johor Bahru.
